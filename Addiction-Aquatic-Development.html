We Found Sushi Paradise In Taiwan: Addiction Aquatic Development

  • March 04, 2018
  • TaiwanFood

Jeremy found this gem on a rainy day as we were looking for unique places to stay dry in Taipei. We are huge sushi fans, so the ADD looked right up our alley.

The quickest way for us to get there was by Uber, but if you aren’t in a rush, Addiction Aquatic Development can be reached by public transportation as well.

When we arrived, we were greeted with fresh fish galore. The outer section of the market is filled with standing tables where you can enjoy your take-out meals. Once you enter the actual market, you may be overwhelmed by the different options, but hopefully in a good way. There are several restaurants inside, but the best deal by far is the take-out section. We shared a box of over 20 salmon and swordfish sashimi for only $320 NTD!

There is also a wide selection of other seafood, veggies, meats, and drinks. We were so full after our sashimi feast, but for the extra hungry there is also a hot pot restaurant on the second floor. Overall, the Addiction Aquatic Development is a must-visit for sashimi and seafood lovers, and I look forward to stuffing my face there again in the future.
